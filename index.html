Top three places to see in Slovakia

Capital City

Bratislava can offer not only very nice castle but also restaurant UFO with wonderful view on Danube river.

Bojnice Castle

After you enjoy the beautiful chambers of Bojnice Castle, you can go to see the ZOO which entrance is right next to the castle.

Slovak Paradise

If you like gorges, ladders over waterfalls, chains, bridges, rivers, caves and hiking, this is definitely right place for you. You will discover that National Park Slovak Paradise is worthy of his name.
